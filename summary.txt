This project analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence on the job market using an interactive dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI.
The dashboard uses an AI job market dataset containing information such as job titles,
salaries, industries, countries, experience levels, and job openings. Various visualizations like a donut chart, area chart,
bar chart, and global map are used to present insights. The dashboard shows salary distribution across roles like AI Engineer,
Data Scientist, and Data Analyst, as well as trends in job openings over the years. It also highlights which industries and countries have the highest demand
for AI professionals. Interactive filters allow users to explore data by education level, company size, and experience. Overall, the project demonstrates the growing demand for 
AI-related jobs and the importance of technical skills such as Machine Learning and Python in the modern workforce.
